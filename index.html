Applications of Mathematics

Welcome, fellow learners, from our young primary students to our wise adults! Here, you will find not how to solve problems, but rather, why we solve them. After all, there is no point learning quadratics in high school if there are no uses for them, right? You may think that we're never gonna use most of the mathematics that we learn. Actually, it turns out that mathematics is prevalent in almost every aspect of our lives, even in jobs that you would not think involve mathematics at all, like a basketball player or an archaeologist! So quit complaining it's useless and go appreciate the beauty of mathematics!
